GST Registration

GST (Goods and Services Tax) is a comprehensive, multi-stage, destination-based tax that is levied on every value addition. It is mandatory for businesses exceeding the prescribed turnover limit to register under GST. Our GST registration service simplifies this process, ensuring full compliance with government regulations while allowing you to focus on growing your business.

Why GST Registration is Essential?

Legal Compliance:

GST registration is mandatory for businesses with an annual turnover exceeding the specified threshold (₹40 lakh for goods and ₹20 lakh for services, varying by state and business type).

Input Tax Credit:

Registered businesses can claim an input tax credit on GST paid on purchases, reducing overall tax liability.

Avoiding Penalties:

Unregistered businesses liable for GST may face heavy penalties, including a fine of 10% of tax due or ₹10,000, whichever is higher.

Business Growth and Expansion:

GST registration allows businesses to expand interstate and participate in government tenders requiring GST compliance.

What We Offer?

Our expert team ensures a seamless GST registration process by handling all legal and compliance requirements. We provide.

1. Eligibility Check:

We assess whether your business meets the GST registration criteria and advise on the best registration approach.

2. Document Collection & Verification:

We guide you in collecting the required documents and verify them to ensure accuracy before submission.

3. Application Filing:

Our professionals fill out the GST application form (GST REG-01) and submit it online via the GST portal.

4. Follow-up with Authorities:

We coordinate with tax authorities to resolve any queries or discrepancies, ensuring a smooth registration process.

5. GSTIN Issuance:

Upon successful verification, we obtain your unique 15-digit GST Identification Number (GSTIN) and provide a GST certificate.

6. Post-Registration Support:

We assist in setting up your GST profile, invoice generation compliance, and filing of initial returns.

Processing Time

The GST registration process typically takes 7-10 working days, depending on the accuracy of submitted documents and government processing time. Any discrepancies may require further clarifications, which we handle on your behalf.
